Fire

What is fire?

a state, process, or instance of combustion in which fuel or other material is ignited and combined with oxygen, giving off light, heat, and flame. a burning mass of material, as on a hearth or in a furnace.

The fire triangle or combustion triangle is a simple model for understanding the necessary ingredients for most fires

The fire tetrahedron represents the addition of a component in the chemical chain reaction, to the three already present in the fire triangle
